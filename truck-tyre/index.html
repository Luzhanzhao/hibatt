How are TBR (radial truck & bus tyres) are being produced?

1. Compounding and mixing all components:  processing of raw materials such as carbon black, natural rubber, synthetic rubber, additives, accelerators, etc. in a compounding machine to produce semi-finished “rubber” products.

2. Tire building: Extrusion, calendering, bead forming, cord truncation, band bundle layer forming.

3. Curing: Put into the tire mold for vulcanization, which already has the appearance of a tire at this time.

4. Final finish: Appearance inspection, uniformity test, dynamic balance test, X-ray inspection

What is Truck Tyre Manufacturing Process?

Raw materials preparation and mixing, Tire building (Extrusion, calendering, bead forming, cord truncation, band bundle layer forming), Curing for vulcanization and finally have tyre looking and checking apprearance, X-RAY, Dynamic Balance Testing.

What kind of raw materials in need to produce the Truck Tyre (TBR)?

Natural rubber, compound rubber, Carbon black, steel wires, additives,etc
