ANSU/WONDERLAND Tires Manufacturer since 2005

ANSU/WONDERLAND Truck Tyres production lines are including TBR, PCR, LTR, Tractor & Trailer tires. The ansu tyre factory is with 40 million square meter and yearly production capacity is around 3.6 million sets. The main tyre brands are ANSU, WONDERLAND, SAFEHOLDER, BYCROSS, etc..

FUJIAN HI-BATT industrial Co,.Ltd has been supplying ANSU tires since last 10 years and we have been working as factory office in Quanzhou. Welcome more clients order quality tyres from China through us.

 

 

ANSU/WONDERLAND Truck Tires Patterns & Sizes

ANSU tire factory has strong R&D engineering team, reliable quality control system, the tyres can be regrooved 2 times under normal road conditons and proper use. The raw materials are A class from Southeast Asian countries. The production lines are advanced equipments from Italy, Germany, Korea, etc..

The tyres have passed certifications such as ECE (R54 and R117), REACH, CCC, DOT, SONCAP, SMARTWAY, SNI, GCC, SASO, NOM, INMETRO, IATF 16949:2016, etc.
